About

Zhi-Quan (Tom) Luo is a Professor and the Vice President at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, as well as the Director of the Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data. His research focuses on optimization methods for signal processing and digital communications, with a recent shift toward data-driven models and data analytics. I aim to combine theoretical foundations with real-world applications to address modern challenges in these fields.

I’m always excited to hear from potential PhD students and postdocs interested in optimization, wireless communication, and artificial intelligence. If you’d like to join our group, please email my assistant with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.
